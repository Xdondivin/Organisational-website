About

History of our company

  • 2003-2009

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Founded in 2003, our organization began as a grassroots initiative focused on providing immediate relief to local communities facing economic hardship. With a small group of volunteers, we launched our first donation drives, addressing needs like food insecurity and housing support. By 2006, our efforts expanded as we formed partnerships with other nonprofits, broadening our reach to support education, healthcare, and disaster relief. Between 2007 and 2009, we solidified our mission and became a recognized leader in grassroots philanthropy, increasing our donor base and launching impactful programs across multiple regions.

  • May/17/2003

    An Agency Was Born

  • December 2015

    Transition to Full Service

    In December 2015, we upgraded our operations to become a full-service donation organization, expanding our programs to offer comprehensive support across education, healthcare, housing, and emergency relief. This transition allowed us to better address the evolving needs of the communities we serve, providing long-term solutions alongside immediate assistance.

  • July 2020

    Phase Two Expansion

    In July 2020, we launched Phase Two of our expansion, significantly increasing our outreach and resources to support global initiatives, including disaster relief, climate action, and economic empowerment programs.
